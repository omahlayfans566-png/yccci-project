About Us

Our Mission & Vision

Young and Chosen for Christ (YCCCI) is a dynamic Catholic youth organization committed to nurturing the spiritual growth of young adults. We provide a welcoming environment where faith comes alive through prayer, fellowship, and service.

Our mission is to empower young adults to deepen their relationship with Christ, build authentic Catholic community, and become witnesses of God's love in the world.
