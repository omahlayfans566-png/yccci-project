Sacred Heart of Jesus Novena

A nine-day novena to the Sacred Heart of Jesus, seeking His abundant love and mercy.

Day 1 of 9

Day 1 - The Love of the Sacred Heart

O Most Holy Heart of Jesus, fountain of every blessing, I adore You, I love You, and with a lively sorrow for my sins, I offer You this poor heart of mine. Make me humble, patient, pure, and wholly obedient to Your will. Grant, good Jesus, that I may live in You and for You. Protect me in the midst of danger; comfort me in my afflictions; give me health of body, assistance in my temporal needs, Your blessing on all that I do, and the grace of a holy death. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Sacred Heart of Jesus, I trust in You.

Day 2 - The Mercy of the Sacred Heart

O Sacred Heart of Jesus, filled with infinite mercy, have compassion on us in our miseries. You are the refuge of sinners, the hope of the despairing, and the comfort of the afflicted. Look upon me with those eyes of mercy which have ever been open to the wretched. Draw me to Your Sacred Heart, and let me find in You my peace, my joy, and my strength. I place all my trust in Your mercy. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Sacred Heart of Jesus, I trust in You.

Day 3 - The Patience of the Sacred Heart

O Sacred Heart of Jesus, meek and humble of heart, teach me to be patient in trials, gentle in adversity, and forgiving toward those who offend me. Your Sacred Heart was pierced with a lance, yet You responded with love. Grant me the grace to bear my crosses with patience and to unite my sufferings with Yours for the salvation of souls. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Sacred Heart of Jesus, I trust in You.

Day 4 - The Humility of the Sacred Heart

O Sacred Heart of Jesus, who humbled Yourself, taking the form of a servant, grant me the grace of true humility. Free me from pride, vanity, and self-love. Help me to recognize my dependence on You and to serve others with a generous heart. May I learn from You, who are gentle and humble of heart, and find rest for my soul. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Sacred Heart of Jesus, I trust in You.

Day 5 - The Generosity of the Sacred Heart

O Sacred Heart of Jesus, infinitely generous, You gave Yourself completely for our salvation. Teach me to be generous in giving my time, talents, and resources for the glory of God and the good of others. Help me to love without counting the cost, to give without expecting return, and to serve with joy. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Sacred Heart of Jesus, I trust in You.

Day 6 - The Peace of the Sacred Heart

O Sacred Heart of Jesus, Prince of Peace, pour Your peace into my heart and into our world. Calm my anxieties, still my fears, and grant me the serenity that comes from trusting in Your divine providence. Heal the divisions in my relationships and in our communities. Make me an instrument of Your peace wherever I go. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Sacred Heart of Jesus, I trust in You.

Day 7 - The Strength of the Sacred Heart

O Sacred Heart of Jesus, fortress of the weak and strength of the weary, be my refuge in times of temptation and trial. When I am weak, You are my strength. When I am discouraged, You are my hope. Fill me with Your courage and fortitude, that I may persevere in faith and remain faithful to Your commandments. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Sacred Heart of Jesus, I trust in You.

Day 8 - The Joy of the Sacred Heart

O Sacred Heart of Jesus, source of true joy, fill my heart with the joy that comes from knowing and loving You. Help me to find my happiness not in worldly things but in Your presence and in doing Your will. May I radiate the joy of the Gospel to everyone I meet, and be a witness to Your love. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Sacred Heart of Jesus, I trust in You.

Day 9 - The Glory of the Sacred Heart

O Sacred Heart of Jesus, King and Center of all hearts, I consecrate myself entirely to You. Reign in my heart, in my home, and in my community. May Your Kingdom come, and may Your will be done in my life as it is in heaven. Grant that I may one day see You face to face and praise Your mercy for all eternity. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Sacred Heart of Jesus, I trust in You.

Conclude with the Litany of the Sacred Heart or the Act of Consecration to the Sacred Heart.

Our Lady of the Rosary Novena

A nine-day novena seeking the intercession of the Blessed Virgin Mary through the Rosary.

Day 1 of 9

Day 1 - The Annunciation

O Blessed Virgin Mary, at the Annunciation you humbly accepted God's will, becoming the Mother of our Savior. Help us to say "yes" to God's plan in our lives with the same faith and trust. Intercede for us that we may be open to the Holy Spirit's guidance and respond generously to God's call. Amen.

Pray one decade of the Rosary (Joyful Mystery: The Annunciation)

Day 2 - The Visitation

O Mary, you traveled in haste to visit your cousin Elizabeth, bringing Jesus to her home. Teach us to bring Christ to others through our words and actions. Help us to be attentive to the needs of those around us and to serve with love and joy. Amen.

Pray one decade of the Rosary (Joyful Mystery: The Visitation)

Day 3 - The Nativity

O Mary, you gave birth to our Savior in humble surroundings. Help us to welcome Jesus into our hearts with simplicity and humility. May we find joy in the simple things and recognize God's presence in the ordinary moments of our lives. Amen.

Pray one decade of the Rosary (Joyful Mystery: The Nativity)

Day 4 - The Presentation

O Mary, you presented Jesus in the Temple, offering Him to the Father. Help us to offer our lives completely to God. Give us the grace to be faithful in our commitments and to trust in God's promises even when we do not fully understand. Amen.

Pray one decade of the Rosary (Joyful Mystery: The Presentation)

Day 5 - The Finding in the Temple

O Mary, you sought Jesus with great sorrow when He was lost, and found Him in the Temple. Help us to seek Jesus diligently when we lose sight of Him in our lives. Grant that we may always find Him in prayer, in Scripture, and in the Eucharist. Amen.

Pray one decade of the Rosary (Joyful Mystery: The Finding in the Temple)

Day 6 - The Agony in the Garden

O Mary, you suffered with Jesus in His agony. Pray for us in our times of trial and distress. Help us to remain faithful in prayer and to accept God's will with trust and surrender, even when the path is difficult. Amen.

Pray one decade of the Rosary (Sorrowful Mystery: The Agony in the Garden)

Day 7 - The Scourging at the Pillar

O Mary, you witnessed the suffering of your Son. Help us to offer our own sufferings in union with Christ for the salvation of souls. Grant us the strength to bear our crosses patiently and to find meaning in our trials. Amen.

Pray one decade of the Rosary (Sorrowful Mystery: The Scourging at the Pillar)

Day 8 - The Crowning with Thorns

O Mary, you stood by Jesus as He was crowned with thorns. Help us to remain faithful when we face humiliation or rejection for our faith. Teach us to see the dignity of every person, especially those who are mocked or marginalized. Amen.

Pray one decade of the Rosary (Sorrowful Mystery: The Crowning with Thorns)

Day 9 - The Resurrection

O Mary, you rejoiced in the Resurrection of your Son. Help us to live as people of hope, confident in the victory of Christ over sin and death. May we be witnesses of the Resurrection, bringing the light of Christ to a world in need of hope. Amen.

Pray one decade of the Rosary (Glorious Mystery: The Resurrection)

St. Joseph Novena

A nine-day novena seeking the intercession of St. Joseph, patron of the universal Church.

Day 1 of 9

Day 1 - St. Joseph, Faithful Guardian

O glorious St. Joseph, faithful guardian of the Holy Family, you protected Jesus and Mary with unwavering devotion. Intercede for us that we may be faithful to our responsibilities and protect those entrusted to our care. Help us to be reliable, trustworthy, and dedicated in all we do. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

St. Joseph, pray for us.

Day 2 - St. Joseph, Model of Silence

O St. Joseph, you spoke few words but your actions spoke volumes. Teach us the value of silence and contemplation. Help us to listen more attentively to God's voice and to respond with quiet obedience and trust. In a noisy world, grant us the grace of interior peace. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

St. Joseph, pray for us.

Day 3 - St. Joseph, Just and Righteous

O St. Joseph, you were a just and righteous man, faithful to God's law. Intercede for us that we may always choose what is right and just in the sight of God. Help us to act with integrity, honesty, and fairness in all our dealings. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

St. Joseph, pray for us.

Day 4 - St. Joseph, Patron of Workers

O St. Joseph, you labored diligently as a carpenter, providing for the Holy Family. Bless the work of our hands and grant us the dignity of meaningful labor. Help us to work with dedication and to offer our work as a prayer to God. Provide for our needs and the needs of our families. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

St. Joseph, pray for us.

Day 5 - St. Joseph, Protector of the Church

O St. Joseph, patron of the universal Church, protect the Church and all her members. Intercede for our Holy Father, our bishops, priests, and religious. Strengthen the faithful and guide those who are searching for the truth. May the Church be a beacon of hope in the world. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

St. Joseph, pray for us.

Day 6 - St. Joseph, Comforter of the Afflicted

O St. Joseph, you experienced anxiety and uncertainty, yet you trusted in God's providence. Comfort those who are anxious, worried, or afraid. Intercede for those who are suffering from financial difficulties, unemployment, or family problems. Grant them peace and trust in God's care. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

St. Joseph, pray for us.

Day 7 - St. Joseph, Model of Fatherhood

O St. Joseph, you were a loving and devoted father to Jesus. Bless all fathers and father figures. Grant them wisdom, patience, and strength to guide their children in faith and virtue. Help them to be examples of love, sacrifice, and integrity. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

St. Joseph, pray for us.

Day 8 - St. Joseph, Hope of the Sick

O St. Joseph, you cared for the Holy Family with tenderness and compassion. Intercede for all who are sick, suffering, or in need of healing. Bring comfort to the afflicted, strength to the weak, and hope to the despairing. May they experience God's healing love through your intercession. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

St. Joseph, pray for us.

Day 9 - St. Joseph, Patron of a Happy Death

O St. Joseph, you died peacefully in the arms of Jesus and Mary. Obtain for us the grace of a holy and peaceful death. Be with us in our final moments, and intercede for us that we may be united with Jesus, Mary, and all the saints in the glory of heaven. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

St. Joseph, pray for us.

Divine Mercy Novena

A nine-day novena given by Jesus to St. Faustina, praying for all souls.

Day 1 of 9

Day 1 - All Mankind, Especially Sinners

"Today bring to Me all mankind, especially all sinners, and immerse them in the ocean of My mercy."

Most Merciful Jesus, whose very nature is compassion and forgiveness, I bring before You all mankind, especially sinners. Immerse them in the ocean of Your mercy. I trust in You.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, and the Divine Mercy Chaplet

Day 2 - The Souls of Priests and Religious

"Today bring to Me the souls of priests and religious, and immerse them in My unfathomable mercy."

Most Merciful Jesus, from whom comes all that is good, I bring before You the souls of priests and religious. Grant them the grace to be faithful shepherds and witnesses of Your mercy. I trust in You.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, and the Divine Mercy Chaplet

Day 3 - All Devout and Faithful Souls

"Today bring to Me all devout and faithful souls, and immerse them in the ocean of My mercy."

Most Merciful Jesus, I bring before You all devout and faithful souls. Strengthen their faith, increase their love, and grant them the grace to persevere in holiness. I trust in You.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, and the Divine Mercy Chaplet

Day 4 - Those Who Do Not Know God

"Today bring to Me those who do not know God, and those who do not yet know Me."

Most Merciful Jesus, I bring before You those who do not know You. Enlighten their minds with the light of faith and draw them to Your Sacred Heart. I trust in You.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, and the Divine Mercy Chaplet

Day 5 - The Souls of Separated Brethren

"Today bring to Me the souls of separated brethren, and immerse them in the ocean of My mercy."

Most Merciful Jesus, I bring before You the souls of our separated brethren. Heal the wounds of division and lead all Christians to unity in Your truth and love. I trust in You.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, and the Divine Mercy Chaplet

Day 6 - The Meek and Humble Souls

"Today bring to Me the meek and humble souls and the souls of little children, and immerse them in My mercy."

Most Merciful Jesus, I bring before You the meek, humble, and little ones. Grant them the grace to grow in holiness and to be a source of blessing to the world. I trust in You.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, and the Divine Mercy Chaplet

Day 7 - The Souls Who Especially Venerate My Mercy

"Today bring to Me the souls who especially venerate and glorify My mercy, and immerse them in My mercy."

Most Merciful Jesus, I bring before You all who venerate Your mercy. Protect them, strengthen them, and fill them with the joy of Your love. I trust in You.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, and the Divine Mercy Chaplet

Day 8 - The Souls in Purgatory

"Today bring to Me the souls who are in the prison of Purgatory, and immerse them in the ocean of My mercy."

Most Merciful Jesus, I bring before You the holy souls in Purgatory. Grant them eternal rest and let perpetual light shine upon them. I trust in You.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, and the Divine Mercy Chaplet

Day 9 - The Souls Who Have Become Lukewarm

"Today bring to Me the souls who have become lukewarm, and immerse them in the abyss of My mercy."

Most Merciful Jesus, I bring before You the lukewarm souls. Rekindle in them the fire of Your love and draw them back to the warmth of Your Sacred Heart. I trust in You.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, and the Divine Mercy Chaplet

Holy Spirit Novena

A nine-day novena invoking the gifts of the Holy Spirit for guidance and renewal.

Day 1 of 9

Day 1 - The Gift of Wisdom

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of Your faithful and kindle in them the fire of Your love. Send forth Your Spirit and they shall be created, and You shall renew the face of the earth.

Grant me the gift of wisdom, that I may see all things through God's eyes and value what is truly important. Help me to love the things of heaven and to live with eternal perspective. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Day 2 - The Gift of Understanding

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of Your faithful and kindle in them the fire of Your love.

Grant me the gift of understanding, that I may penetrate the mysteries of faith and grasp the deeper meaning of Scripture and Tradition. Enlighten my mind to know God more intimately. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Day 3 - The Gift of Counsel

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of Your faithful and kindle in them the fire of Your love.

Grant me the gift of counsel, that I may make wise decisions in accordance with God's will. Guide me in times of uncertainty and help me to choose what is right and good. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Day 4 - The Gift of Fortitude

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of Your faithful and kindle in them the fire of Your love.

Grant me the gift of fortitude, that I may have the courage to live my faith boldly and to persevere in the face of trials and opposition. Strengthen my resolve to follow Christ. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Day 5 - The Gift of Knowledge

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of Your faithful and kindle in them the fire of Your love.

Grant me the gift of knowledge, that I may know God's will and purpose for my life. Help me to see Your hand in all creation and to use the things of this world for Your glory. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Day 6 - The Gift of Piety

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of Your faithful and kindle in them the fire of Your love.

Grant me the gift of piety, that I may worship God with filial love and devotion. Deepen my prayer life and help me to approach God as a loving Father with confidence and trust. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Day 7 - The Gift of Fear of the Lord

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of Your faithful and kindle in them the fire of Your love.

Grant me the gift of fear of the Lord, that I may have a profound reverence for God's majesty and a deep desire to avoid anything that would offend Him. Fill me with awe at Your greatness. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Day 8 - The Fruits of the Holy Spirit

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of Your faithful and kindle in them the fire of Your love.

Grant me the fruits of the Holy Spirit: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control. May these fruits be evident in my life and draw others to You. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Day 9 - Renewal of the Spirit

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of Your faithful and kindle in them the fire of Your love.

Renew in me the grace of my Baptism and Confirmation. Fill me afresh with Your presence and power. Send me forth as a witness of the Gospel, and use me to build up the Kingdom of God. Amen.

Pray: Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Veni, Sancte Spiritus! (Come, Holy Spirit!)

Immaculate Conception Novena

A nine-day novena honoring the Immaculate Conception of the Blessed Virgin Mary.

Day 1 of 9

Day 1 - The Immaculate Conception

O Immaculate Virgin, conceived without sin, you are the masterpiece of God's grace. From the first moment of your existence, you were preserved from the stain of original sin. Intercede for us that we may be purified from our sins and grow in holiness. Help us to imitate your purity and your complete surrender to God's will. Amen.

Pray: Hail Mary (3 times), Glory Be

Day 2 - Mary, Full of Grace

O Mary, full of grace, the Lord is with you. You were filled with the Holy Spirit from the first moment of your existence. Obtain for us the grace to be filled with God's presence and to live in a state of grace. Help us to cooperate with the graces God offers us each day. Amen.

Pray: Hail Mary (3 times), Glory Be

Day 3 - Mary, Blessed Among Women

O Blessed Virgin, all generations call you blessed. You are the most blessed of all women because of your faith and your yes to God. Intercede for us that we may be counted among the blessed who hear the word of God and keep it. Amen.

Pray: Hail Mary (3 times), Glory Be

Day 4 - Mary, Mother of God

O Mary, Mother of God, you gave birth to the Savior of the world while remaining a virgin. Your divine motherhood is the source of all your privileges. Intercede for us that we may be faithful children of the Church and that we may always turn to you as our mother. Amen.

Pray: Hail Mary (3 times), Glory Be

Day 5 - Mary, Handmaid of the Lord

O Mary, you humbly declared yourself the handmaid of the Lord. Your humility drew God's favor upon you. Teach us the beauty of humility and help us to surrender our lives completely to God's will, trusting in His plan for us. Amen.

Pray: Hail Mary (3 times), Glory Be

Day 6 - Mary, Seat of Wisdom

O Mary, Seat of Wisdom, you pondered the mysteries of God in your heart. Intercede for us that we may grow in wisdom and understanding of the faith. Help us to seek knowledge that leads to holiness and to use our minds for the glory of God. Amen.

Pray: Hail Mary (3 times), Glory Be

Day 7 - Mary, Queen of Angels

O Mary, Queen of Angels, the heavenly hosts honor and serve you. Send your angels to protect and guide us. May we, like the angels, always behold the face of God and do His will with joy and promptness. Amen.

Pray: Hail Mary (3 times), Glory Be

Day 8 - Mary, Refuge of Sinners

O Mary, Refuge of Sinners, you are the safe haven for all who seek God's mercy. Intercede for us who are sinners, that we may receive the grace of true repentance and conversion. Lead us to the Sacrament of Reconciliation and to a renewed life in Christ. Amen.

Pray: Hail Mary (3 times), Glory Be

Day 9 - Mary, Queen of Heaven

O Mary, Queen of Heaven, you were assumed into heaven and crowned with glory. Intercede for us that we may one day join you in the eternal joy of heaven. Help us to live each day with our eyes fixed on our heavenly home. Amen.

Pray: Hail Mary (3 times), Glory Be

O Mary, conceived without sin, pray for us who have recourse to you.
